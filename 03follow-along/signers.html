Who Were the Signers

Famous Signers

Some of the most famous signers of the Declaration of Independence include

Thomas Jefferson
The primary author of the document and later the third President of the United States.
John Adams
A strong advocate for independence who became the second U.S. President.
Benjamin Franklin
A renowned inventor, diplomat, and statesman, who played a key role in securing French support for the American Revolution.
John Hancock
President of the Second Continental Congress, known for his large and bold signature.
Samuel Adams
A leader of the Sons of Liberty and a major force behind the push for independence.

This list highlights some of the most influential figures who signed the Declaration of Independence. Their courage and leadership played a crucial role in shaping the United States, laying the foundation for the freedoms and principles the nation upholds today. Each of these individuals contributed uniquely to the cause of independence, leaving a lasting legacy in American history.
