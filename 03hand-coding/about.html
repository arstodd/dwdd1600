About Us

Who We Are

We are a website dedicated to providing a comprehensive guide to Dungeons and Dragons ™ Whether you're brand new to the game or a seasoned adventurer looking to sharpen your skills, our website is your trusted companion for all things D&D. From beginner-friendly guides and character creation tips to deep dives into rules, storytelling, and strategy, we are here to help you roll with confidence and bring your imagination to life.

About Our Founder

This website was created by Alex Stoddard. He began playing D&D in 2018 and is still playing today. He has created multiple campaigns and characters for D&D. We strive to emulate his love for the game so that others can enjoy it as well.
