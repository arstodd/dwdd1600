The Forgotten Realms

The Official Setting of Dungeons and Dragons ™

The Forgotten Realms is a fantasy world filled with magic, powerful gods, ancient ruins, and diverse cultures. It is one of the most well-known settings in Dungeons & Dragons. Most adventures take place on the continent of Faerûn, which is home to bustling cities like Waterdeep, dangerous wilderness areas, and secretive underground realms like the Underdark.

Realms are shaped by conflicts between heroes and villains, gods and mortals, and kingdoms and monsters. Magic is common, and powerful wizards, clerics, and adventurers play major roles in shaping the world’s history.

Created by Ed Greenwood and developed over many editions of D&D, the Forgotten Realms offers a rich setting for any kind of fantasy adventure, from epic battles to political intrigue.

When Creating Your Character

Consider including:

  • Deep Lore from Faerûn, including cities like Waterdeep, Baldur’s Gate, and Neverwinter
  • Character Creation rooted in Forgotten Realms races, classes, and backgrounds
  • Play D&D 5e in the context of the Realms' politics, deities, and history

Create With Friends

Include your friends for a more immersive experience. Here's what you can talk think about while reading.

  1. Your relationship. Are you friends, family, or coworkers?
  2. What major event forced you to work together?
  3. What classes and backgrounds compliment each other? How can they relate?
